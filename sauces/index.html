Brown Butter

For all flavors

Melt 3 tablespoons butter in a saucepan over medium heat and cook approx. 1-2 minutes, allowing it to bubble and foam. When the butter turns light brown and has a nutty aroma, turn the heat off immediately – do not allow it to burn.

Sage Butter

For Chanterelle, Beet, and Squash

Follow recipe above for Brown Butter. When the butter has melted in the pan and before it begins to bubble, add 2-3 fresh sage leaves. The crisped sage leaves can be used as a garnish.

Cumin Crème Fraîche

For Carrot & Cumin

Toast 1 teaspoon ground cumin in a saucepan on medium heat until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add ½ cup crème fraîche and ½ tablespoon lemon juice and stir to combine. Thin the mixture slightly with a few tablespoons of water. Salt to taste.

Horseradish Crème Fraîche

For Beef Short Rib

In a saucepan, combine ½ cup crème fraîche and 2 tablespoons creamed horseradish and melt over medium-low heat. Thin with butter or ravioli water to desired consistency.

Garlic & Tomato

For Eggplant, Tallegio & Cream, Swiss Chard & Ricotta

Heat 3 tablespoons olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Add 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced, and cook until golden brown. Remove garlic from pan, finely chop, and set aside.

Slice 3 ripe tomatoes into quarters, removing the stems. Add to pan and sauté until soft. Off the heat, add the reserved garlic. Salt and pepper to taste.

Caramelized Onion & Butter

For Ricotta & Parmesan

In a large saucepan, heat 1 jar good caramelized onions with 1 tablespoon butter. Thin with ravioli water as needed.

Bread Crumb & Pancetta

For Pancetta & Roasted Onion

Preheat oven to 375°. In a food processor, pulse 2 slices hearty white bread into coarse crumbs (about 10 pulses – do not purée). Toss crumbs with 2 tablespoons olive oil and a dash of salt and pepper. Spread the mixture on a rimmed baking sheet and bake, stirring often, until golden, about 8 minutes.

In a large saucepan, heat ⅓ cup olive oil over medium heat. Add ¼ pound diced pancetta and cook until golden, 1-2 minutes.

Turn up the heat slightly, stir in breadcrumbs, and cook for 10 seconds. Season with salt and pepper.
